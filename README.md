## 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis

The **Pizza Sales Analysis Project** is a business intelligence and data analytics project focused on analyzing pizza sales data to uncover valuable insights into revenue generation, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and overall business growth. The project combines **SQL, Excel, and Power BI** to transform raw transactional data into meaningful visual reports that support strategic decision-making. 

The analysis process begins with **Excel**, where the dataset is cleaned, validated, and prepared for analysis. SQL is then used to query and aggregate sales data, calculate key performance indicators (KPIs), and identify important business trends. The processed data is finally visualized in **Power BI** through an interactive dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance and operational metrics. 

The dashboard enables users to monitor critical metrics such as **Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, Sales Trends, Best-Selling Pizzas, Category Performance, and Customer Ordering Patterns**. Through interactive visualizations and filters, stakeholders can easily explore sales performance across different products and time periods, helping identify top-performing items and opportunities for business improvement. 

### Key Features

* 🍕 Sales and Revenue Performance Analysis
* 📊 Interactive Power BI Dashboard
* 🗄️ SQL-Based Data Querying and Aggregation
* 📈 Sales Trend and Order Pattern Analysis
* 🏆 Best-Selling Pizza Identification
* 📋 Category and Product Performance Insights
* 🧹 Data Cleaning and Validation Using Excel
* 📉 KPI Tracking and Business Reporting

This project demonstrates my ability to work with **data cleaning, SQL analytics, dashboard development, and business intelligence tools** to convert raw sales data into actionable insights that support informed business decisions and performance optimization.
